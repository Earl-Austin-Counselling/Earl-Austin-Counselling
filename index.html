About Earl

I am a qualified counsellor offering a supportive, non‑judgemental environment where you can talk openly and at your own pace. My work is grounded in empathy, respect, and a commitment to helping you make sense of what you’re experiencing.

Services

  • One‑to‑one counselling (in‑person or online)
  • Support with anxiety, stress, and low mood
  • Trauma‑informed practice
  • Relationship and family concerns
  • Workplace stress and burnout

My Approach

I work integratively, drawing from person‑centred and evidence‑based therapeutic models. Sessions are collaborative and paced according to your needs. You decide what feels safe to explore, and I walk alongside you with care and curiosity.
