About Earl

I offer counselling that is steady, respectful, and rooted in real life. Together we can explore patterns, pressures, and past experiences at a pace that feels manageable.

My approach is collaborative and trauma-informed, with an emphasis on safety, choice, and curiosity rather than judgement.

How we might work together

Sessions are shaped around you, but here are some of the areas people often bring.

Anxiety, stress and overwhelm

Making sense of constant pressure, worry, or burnout, and finding steadier ways to respond.

Loss, change and transition

Grief, endings, and life changes that leave you feeling unmoored or unsure of who you are now.

Relationships and boundaries

Exploring patterns in relationships, people‑pleasing, or difficulty saying no without guilt.

Trauma and difficult histories

Working gently with past experiences that still shape how safe, seen, or worthy you feel today.
